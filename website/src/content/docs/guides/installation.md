ELRS Mobile is available on official app stores for the most seamless experience and automatic updates.

## Official App Stores

### Android
**Google Play Store**: The application is available for all modern Android devices.
[**Install from Google Play**](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.datarx.elrsmobile)

### iOS
**Apple TestFlight**: Join the public beta to install ELRS Mobile on your iPhone or iPad.
[**Join TestFlight Beta**](https://testflight.apple.com/join/jwU1WrpX)

## Manual Installation
For power users or those who prefer manual management, visit our [**GitHub Releases**](https://github.com/wbhinton/ELRS-Mobile/releases) page for the latest `.apk` files.
